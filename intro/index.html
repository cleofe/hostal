Hostal Cleofé Arequipa

(en español)

Located in the traditional neighbourhood of San Lazáro, the birthplace of Arequipa. The area has been declared a World Heritage Site by UNESCO and preserves much colonial architecture including the first plaza of the city.

At hostal Cleofé you will enjoy a quiet, pleasant stay and personal service.
