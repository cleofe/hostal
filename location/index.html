Address || Hostal Cleofé

(en español)

Pasaje Ripacha 105-A,
Barrio San Lázaro
Arequipa, Peru

Hostal Clefé is conveniently located just a few blocks from the main tourist sights such as the Santa Catalina Monastery and the Plaza de Armas.

Across the street from the Hostal is the oldest bakery in town, where they continue to bake their delicious bread in a 150 year old wood oven.
